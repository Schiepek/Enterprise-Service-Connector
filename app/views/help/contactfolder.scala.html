You have 2 options to configure your Contact Transfer from Salesforce to Google

  • Transfer contacts to your personal contacts folder. With this option you are able to edit and delete contacts in your Google account. If somebody calls you, the usernames appears on your smartphone.

  • Transfer contacts to de directory folder of your Google enterprise account. All users of the company are able to see the Salesforce contacts in the google account. Edit and delete of contacts in the directory folder is not possible. To delete all the Salesforce contacts in Google you have use the delete button.
